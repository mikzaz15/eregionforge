# Moat and Strategy

## Core moat
The moat is not LLM usage. The moat is the compounding, revisioned, evidence-linked canonical knowledge layer.

## Defensible layers
1. compiled corpus
2. workflow compounding
3. trust infrastructure
4. domain packs
5. knowledge operations and linting

## Best wedge
Investment research is the strongest initial commercial wedge because synthesis, traceability, and recurring monitoring have clear economic value.

## Failure modes
- drifting into generic chat-with-files
- becoming a generic notes app
- weak evidence discipline
- chasing enterprise breadth too early
