# Product Vision

## What EregionForge is
EregionForge is a knowledge compiler for serious research.

It turns messy source material into a living research wiki that is:
- structured
- auditable
- revisioned
- evidence-linked
- continuously improvable

## Who it is for
Primary initial user:
- solo analyst, founder, investor, legal researcher, or technical researcher

Later:
- research teams
- funds
- legal teams
- diligence teams
- domain-intelligence functions

## The user problem
Users can collect documents and ask questions, but they still do not get:
- a canonical view of what is known
- a durable body of knowledge
- visible contradictions and gaps
- revision history for changing understanding
- outputs that compound over time

## Product promise
Turn source chaos into a living research wiki.

## Product principles
1. The compiled layer is the product.
2. Evidence should be visible.
3. Outputs must persist as assets.
4. Contradictions should surface, not disappear.
5. New sources should trigger maintenance and improvement.
6. Markdown is first-class.

## Long-term vision
A user asks a difficult question. EregionForge refreshes sources, recompiles affected pages, checks contradictions, writes a report, and files that report back into the knowledge base.
